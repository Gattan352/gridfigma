The asset

Understanding the asset

The asset typically contains 3 layers:

The grid lines

Grid blocks

The reveal mask

The reveal mask is responsible for showing the asset. The mask contains blobs which act as alpha (visibility layer) for the grids.

The mask contains blobs which act as alpha (visibility layer) for the grids.

How to use

Step 1

Bring the asset frame into your design frame obviously

Step 2

Place it in the appropriate layer (I place mine behind all of the webpage content as shown)

Step 3

Modify it as you wish (Ensure to lock other layers to work in a non-destructive way)

– More on how to edit in the highlighted sections below

Move the blobs around. Place them where you want the grids shown, remove them from where you don’t want the grids shown.

Rotate and resize blobs to change how the grids show (the possibilities are endless)

If you are comfortable with the pen tool, change the shape of the blobs to change how the grids are shown
